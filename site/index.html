GEOSTORY

A Geospacial Analysis of the Passions of Art Historians

Introduction

“Travel is about the gorgeous feeling of teetering in the unknown.”-Anthony Bourdain

GEOSTORY is a geospatial analysis of the digital archive Artchives with the aim of exploring relationships between the country of birth and citizenship of art historians and the location of collections from a distributional point of view.

Art history is a discipline highly affected by the cultural background of the artistic periods, artworks, collections and art historians. But to what extent does culture affect artistic studies? Are there any stereotypes and preconceptions that influence research? After a keen analysis of data from Artchives and Wikidata, we have discovered, for instance that:

Italy is a country with a rich cultural and artistic heritage and the majority of cultural institutions and collections can be found there. However, the majority of art historians are from Germany.

The team of Artelling invites you to explore our data in this visual journey!
